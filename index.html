Psychology / Numerical Cognition / Neuroimaging

Research on multiplication fluency, memory, and learning.

I am a psychologist and recent PhD graduate whose work brings together behavioural experiments, large-sample cognitive data, and neuroimaging to understand how arithmetic fluency develops and varies across individuals.

This site brings together my research agenda, publications, academic updates, and opportunities for collaboration in cognitive, educational, and methods-driven psychology.
